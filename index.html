All Foods

Market analysis for Australian grocery store chain aimed at increasing profits.

All Foods has been a staple for many when it comes to groceries. However, recent changes to operating hours have resulted in a loss of $50,000 in revenue as well as $40,000 in profit. Data analysis was performed, yielding several findings and conclusions on profitable merchandise and consumer tendencies.
